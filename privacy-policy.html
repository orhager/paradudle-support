Privacy Policy

Effective: March 23, 2026

Paradudle does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your practice stays on your device.

What We Don't Collect

Paradudle does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

Data Stored on Your Device

Paradudle stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage frameworks:

  • Practice sessions — start time, duration, tempo, segments, and any notes you write. Stored via SwiftData.
  • Settings — your preferred sound kit, daily goal, session defaults, and tempo. Stored via UserDefaults.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

No Network Requests

Paradudle makes zero network requests. The app works entirely offline. There are no servers, no APIs, no cloud sync.

No Third-Party SDKs

Paradudle contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. The app is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Paradudle does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. There is no account creation, no data submission, and no tracking of any kind.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
